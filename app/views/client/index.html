                Skill Group
              

              
            

            

              

                Skill Group is determined by performance metrics collected over
                recent weeks, with more recent activities receiving greater
                weight. Additional variables such as time spent and workload
                also influence weighting.
              

              

                Your Skill Group can only be recalibrated every week. The
                "Recalibrate" button will appear when available. Keep in mind
                that your latest performance may not be reflected until the
                following week.
